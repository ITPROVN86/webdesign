About the Competition

The WEB DESIGN INNOVATION 2026 competition aims to foster creativity and innovation in web UI/UX design, emphasizing the integration of AI technologies to enhance user experiences. This event bridges academic learning with industry practices, connecting students with technology enterprises for real-world applications.

Purpose

To promote innovative web design solutions that leverage AI for improved user interactions and intelligent interfaces.

Focus Areas

Web UI/UX design combined with AI applications, emphasizing user-centric solutions over technical demonstrations.

Connection

Building bridges between universities and enterprises to prepare students for the future of intelligent web design.
